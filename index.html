Ok, so here is the gist of the game. The total score that you want to achieve is under the Random Total Generated on the top right under these instructions.

Each of the four crystals has been assigned a random number between one and twelve. If the random numbers generated are all ever and you happen to have an odd total number....well sorry but you cannot win.

When you win it will keep track of total wins, same with the losses.

I will work on improving the graphics but it took a lot longer than anticipated just to get the gameplay working correctly....and ulitmately it is free to play.
